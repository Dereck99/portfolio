This is my tribute page for Peter Clemenza from the Godfather movie. Clemenza was a loyal Caporegime for the Corleone family. He was one of Vito Corleone's closest friends and helped him enter a life of crime as a way to make money when Vito lost his job to Fanuiccis nephew. Clemenza was both brutal and intelligent. Vito felt he could trust him to not take things too far when a "message" needed to be sent. I have some pictures of him and a list of things that he is known for.

    Here is a list of some of the things that Peter is known for

  • One of Don Vito Corleones Caporegimes
  • One of Don Vito Corleanes oldest friends and Godfather to Vito's son Santino "Sonny"
  • Clemenza was a great leader and judge of talent. He personally groomed 5 future Caporegimes in the Corleone Family
  • Counciled Michael Corleone on how to kill Sollozzo and Captain Mark McCluskey
  • Ordered and helped with the murder of Pauli Gatto for his part in the attempted assasination of Don Vito Corleone
  • Personally executed Don Victor Stracci. The head of the Stracci crime family of New York
  • Personally executed Carlo Rizzi for his part in the Sonny Corleone assasination
  • Died under supsicious cercomstances

The character, Peter Clemenza, has appeared in two of the Godfather Movies, three Godfather novels and one Godfather video game.
